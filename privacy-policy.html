Privacy Policy

Welcome to KathaLok. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

When you use KathaLok, we may collect the following information:

  • Account Information: If you log in via Google or Facebook, we collect your name, email, and profile picture. Anonymous users will have a randomly generated identifier.
  • Stories & Interactions: Stories you write, likes, shares, and follows are stored to improve user experience.
  • Device Information: We collect device details to ensure app compatibility.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Enable login and account management.
  • Display and personalize content based on user interactions.
  • Improve the app experience and provide better recommendations.
  • Ensure security and prevent fraudulent activity.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. However, we may share limited information:

  • With third-party services (Google/Facebook) for authentication.
  • If required by law enforcement or government authorities.

4. Data Security

We implement encryption and security measures to protect user data. However, no system is 100% secure.

5. Your Choices

You can edit or delete your profile at any time. For data removal requests, contact our support.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy. We will notify users about significant changes.
